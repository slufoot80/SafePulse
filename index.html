Everything You Need to Stay Safe

Powerful safety features designed to protect you and give your loved ones peace of mind.

🚨

SOS Emergency Button

One tap sends an immediate alert with your location to all your emergency contacts. When seconds matter, SafePulse delivers.

👥

Safety Circles

Create trusted groups of family and friends who receive your check-in status and alerts. Stay connected to the people who care.

🔋

Battery Warning Alerts

If your phone's battery gets dangerously low, SafePulse automatically warns your circle so they know you may go offline soon.

📢

Loud Alarm & Auto-Call

When you trigger an emergency, SafePulse sounds a loud bystander alarm and can automatically dial your first emergency contact — no extra taps needed.

📚

Journaling & Mood Tracking

Track your emotional well-being over time. Reflect on your day and spot patterns in your mental health journey.

💚

Wellness & Mental Health

Receive gentle wellness nudges and access mental health resources. SafePulse cares about your whole well-being, not just emergencies.
